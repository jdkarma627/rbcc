# RBCC Sponsorship Proposal

## Royal Bengal Cricket Club (RBCC) - Sponsorship Proposal 2026

### About RBCC

Royal Bengal Cricket Club (RBCC) is a community-driven cricket organization based in Alberta, committed to promoting cricket, fitness, youth development, and social connection. Our activities bring together players, families, and local communities through regular matches, tournaments, and social events.

We are currently reviving RBCC as an Alberta Society, aiming to build a sustainable, structured, and community-focused sports organization.

---

## Our Vision

- Promote cricket and active lifestyles year-round
- Build a strong, inclusive community
- Develop youth through structured sports programs
- Create a positive and professional club environment
