2026-02-05, 9:42 p.m. - JD: *Team Structure Proposal for 2026*

We're not closing teams. We're restructuring how we think about them.

`Current problem:` most members want competitive cricket but won't commit to the work that requires. 

We can't fix that by managing A/B teams the same way we always have.

*New approach:*

`Four format-specific teams`

T20 Competitive
T20 Developmental
T40 Competitive
T40 Developmental

*How it works from day one:*

At the start of the season, we divide the entire playing member pool into four groups of 7-8 players. 

Each player gets assigned to one team as their home base. 

That's where they belong, where they build chemistry, where they focus their energy until mid-season.

Can you still play for other teams if needed? Yes.

But your primary commitment, your practice focus, your selection priority is with your home team. This creates actual team identity instead of the current "whoever shows up" approach.

Players pick their format priority during pre-season. Selection into each group happens based on:

- Practice participation
- Fitness
- Last season performance
- Club involvement (circus, chat, admin, other activities)
- Player availability and stated commitment
- Requirements and balance of the team (dont need 7 all-rounders per team)

Captains and common selector determine the weight of each critera. Mid-season, we reassess and redistribute based on the same framework.

*What this means practically:*

If you want T20 Competitive, expect weekday games and minimum one practice per week. That's the minimum + whatever expectations are set by the captains. 

If you can't commit to that, you belong in T20 Developmental, where the expectation is different.

Performance in T40 doesn't earn you a T20 Competitive spot. You prove yourself in the format you want to play. 

If you want to move up from T20 Developmental to T20 Competitive, you do it through T20 performance and commitment, not cross-format reputation.

This gives everyone a clear path. It also stops the situation where someone gets picked for competitive play based on potential or past performance but won't show up for practice. <This message was edited>
