## Project Overview

Royal Bengal Cricket Club (RBCC) is a registered Alberta non-profit cricket organization based in Edmonton, competing in the Edmonton & District Cricket League (EDCL). Founded in 2016, RBCC serves the Bangladeshi-Canadian community while promoting competitive cricket development. Key achievements include winning the BCAOC Champions Trophy in Calgary (February 2026) undefeated, with standout performances from players like Mousum Dhar and John Karmaker.

The club fields two teams (Team A in Premier Division, Team B in lower divisions) and emphasizes community service, multi-sport participation, and professional networking. Executive leadership includes roles such as President (Abdul Hamid Sumon), VP Finance (John Karmaker), and others, with a focus on structured governance via bylaws and annual AGMs.

This workspace documents RBCC's strategic plans, communications, and operational files as of March 2026.

## Marketing Skills Integration

RBCC now includes a curated set of local marketing skills under .github/skills. These skills are task-focused instruction packs used by the AI agent to improve quality and consistency for planning, copy, campaigns, and measurement.

### Active Skills (First Rollout)

- **product-marketing-context**: Foundation context. Use first for positioning, audience definitions, and value proposition.
- **social-content**: Weekly and monthly social planning, post drafts, and content batching.
- **copywriting**: Sponsor pitch copy, website page copy, CTAs, and campaign messaging.
- **paid-ads**: Campaign structure, creative direction, targeting ideas, and offer framing.
- **site-architecture**: Website page map, navigation priorities, and information flow.
- **sales-enablement**: Sponsor one-pagers, proposal support, and objection responses.
- **analytics-tracking**: Measurement plans, UTM strategy, and reporting structure.
- **email-sequence**: Sponsor and member nurture flows with clear sequence goals.
- **cold-email**: Sponsor outreach drafts and follow-up cadence.
- **marketing-psychology**: Message angle refinement using trust, social proof, and motivation triggers.
- **competitor-alternatives**: Structured RBCC vs competitor positioning and differentiation.
- **page-cro**: Conversion-focused review and rewrite of landing and campaign pages.

### How to Use These Skills

- Ask naturally with a concrete output request, target audience, and channel.
- Mention RBCC context explicitly (sports club, non-profit structure, community-first brand, Edmonton focus).
- For cross-functional work, request combined outputs (for example: copywriting + social-content + analytics-tracking in one workflow).
- Use Marketing_Plans/rbcc_skill_prompt_templates.md as the default starter library for recurring tasks before drafting new prompts.

Example prompts:

- "Create a 4-week Instagram and Facebook calendar for RBCC with player spotlight, matchday, and sponsor appreciation posts."
- "Rewrite the sponsor pitch intro for a Title Partner audience and include two CTA options."
- "Design a tracking plan for sponsor page visits, sponsorship inquiries, and conversion reporting."

### How Skills Should Be Improved

- **After each major use**: Save successful prompts and output patterns in Marketing_Plans/ notes.
- **Monthly review**: Identify which skills produced usable first drafts vs which needed heavy editing.
- **Quarterly review**: Keep, replace, or expand skills based on campaign outcomes and sponsor conversion impact.
- **Upgrade candidates**: content-strategy, launch-strategy, and ab-test-setup if workload expands.

Primary source library used for this rollout: https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills

## Maintenance Guidelines

- **Adding Files**: Place new documents in the most appropriate subfolder (e.g., marketing updates in Marketing_Plans/). Update this Readme.md with summaries and relationships.
- **Updating Content**: Revise sections as initiatives progress or new info emerges. Keep personal use in mind—no public sharing.
- **Automatic Updates**: When specific instructions or tasks are provided, agents will automatically update this Readme.md to reflect changes in initiatives, documents, or structure without requiring reminders.
- **Skills Maintenance**: Keep active skill folders in .github/skills aligned with current initiatives. Remove inactive skills and document changes here.
- **Skills Quality Loop**: Track prompt quality, revision effort, and outcome quality for each skill; use monthly and quarterly reviews to improve selection.
- **Prompt Template Maintenance**: After major campaigns, log the best-performing prompts in Marketing_Plans/rbcc_skill_prompt_templates.md and review quarterly for updates.
- **Archiving**: Move outdated files to Archives/ and note in Readme.md.
- **Clean Root**: Ensure the root directory remains minimal; use subfolders for organization.
- **Language Notes**: Avoid using the word "fostering"; prefer alternatives like "promoting" or "advancing".
