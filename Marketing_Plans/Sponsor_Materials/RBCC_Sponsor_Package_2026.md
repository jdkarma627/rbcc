# RBCC Marketing and Advertising Partner Package 2026

## About This Partnership

Royal Bengal Cricket Club is Edmonton's reigning BCAOC Champions Trophy winner, a registered Alberta non-profit, and home to 38+ active members — most of them working professionals with graduate-level education across government, tech, and business.

We run two competitive teams in the Edmonton & District Cricket League, host community events year-round, and reach a professional Bangladeshi-Canadian audience that traditional advertising cannot access. In 2026 alone, RBCC will deliver 52+ branded touchpoints through matches, tournaments, community events, newsletters, and social media.

This is not a donation request. This is a marketing and advertising partnership with defined deliverables, written agreements, and evidence-based reporting. Campaign design, content production, and sponsor reporting are handled by qualified professionals.

## Why Local Businesses Choose RBCC

**Your audience is already here.** RBCC members and their networks are engineers, IT professionals, researchers, public servants, and entrepreneurs. Your brand reaches decision-makers across multiple industries — not just cricket spectators.

**Year-round visibility, not a one-day logo.** With 40+ league matches, 12+ community events, tournament travel, newsletter editions, and social media coverage, your brand stays active across 12 months. At the Title tier, that works out to roughly $192 per branded touchpoint — less than a single local newspaper ad, reaching a more targeted audience.

**Proven track record.** RBCC won the 2026 BCAOC Champions Trophy in Calgary, finishing undefeated across all 5 matches. Six RBCC players ranked in the tournament's top 25 MVPs. The club fields a former Canada national team representative and current EDCL selector.

**Professional execution.** Marketing materials, campaign assets, and reporting are produced by professionals — not put together by volunteers between innings. You receive structured quarterly reports with visibility evidence, not a handshake and a hope.

### Title Marketing Partner — $10,000/year (Exclusive)

One partner. Maximum visibility. Fully integrated into the club's public presence.

- Primary logo placement on all sponsor-facing club materials
- Naming rights for up to 2 major events (e.g., "[Your Brand] Bengal Cup")
- Priority visibility on match-day social posts and event coverage
- Dedicated spotlight in every newsletter edition
- On-ground branding at all approved matches, tournaments, and events
- Branded presence at Royal Canadian Circus volunteer nights (June — 500+ attendees per night)
- Named recognition at AGM, Gala Night, and community gatherings
- Quarterly impact report with delivered activities and visibility evidence

**What your $10K funds:** League registration for both teams, training facilities, tournament entry, team equipment — the operational backbone that keeps RBCC competing and visible.

### Growth Partner — $5,000/year

Strong season-long visibility with meaningful event integration.

- Prominent logo placement in sponsor sections across club materials
- Naming rights for 1 event
- Recurring mentions in match-day social posts and newsletter editions
- Event-day branding at approved matches and community events
- End-of-season summary report

**What your $5K funds:** Event operations, travel support for inter-city tournaments, and community programming costs.

### Community Partner — $2,000/year

Entry-level visibility for local businesses looking for direct community connection.

- Logo in community partner sections of club materials
- Quarterly sponsor acknowledgment posts on social channels
- Newsletter mention in sponsor block each edition
- Invitation and acknowledgment at selected club events
- End-of-season summary report

**What your $2K funds:** Practice facilities, junior development programming, and community event costs.
