# RBCC Sponsor Activation Calendar 2026 (v2)

## Purpose

This calendar shows partners and members what sponsor visibility looks like month by month. It sets realistic expectations and connects each activation to a proof artifact. Final dates are confirmed by RBCC event and operations leads.

## Activation Guardrails

These rules protect both RBCC and the partner from overcommitment:

- Do not commit event naming rights until event ownership and scheduling are confirmed.
- Do not commit banner dimensions, print quantities, or booth setups unless materials are resourced and budgeted.
- Do not schedule sponsor speaking segments unless the event agenda is finalized and approved.
- Every activation must have an assigned owner and a proof artifact before it counts as delivered.
- If an activation cannot be completed as planned, notify the partner in writing and propose a make-up action.

## Evidence Standards

Every completed activation must produce at least one evidence record:

| Evidence Type | Applies To |
| --- | --- |
| Social media post link (with screenshot) | Match-day mentions, announcement posts, event coverage |
| Newsletter screenshot or PDF | Newsletter sponsor blocks, spotlight features |
| Event photo with visible branding | On-ground signage, jersey branding, volunteer events |
| Written acknowledgment note | AGM recognition, speech mentions, welcome messages |

## Operational Notes

- Payment status must be verified before any activation begins.
- If event scope changes mid-season, update the partner in writing before executing revised deliverables.
- Missed activations require a documented catch-up plan shared with the partner within 7 days.
