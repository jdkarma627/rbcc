## 6. Brand and Content Usage Rights
*How logos and brand materials can be used.*

- RBCC may use partner logos only for agreed activations described in this agreement.
- Partner grants RBCC non-exclusive use of logos for campaign and event materials within the agreement term.
- Any use of partner logos beyond the scope described here requires separate written approval.
- RBCC will not modify, recolor, or distort partner logos without written permission.

## 7. Scope and Governance
*What sponsors can and can't direct.*

- Partners receive the advertising and visibility deliverables defined in this agreement. RBCC's internal budget allocation and spending decisions remain a club governance matter.
- Requests outside the signed scope require a written amendment to this agreement before activation.
- Verbal commitments by any party are not binding. All changes must be documented in writing.
- RBCC reserves the right to decline requests that conflict with club bylaws, governance policies, or existing partner agreements.

## 9. Renewal and Priority
*How the partnership continues or ends.*

- Renewal discussions open at least 60 days before the term end date.
- Tier exclusivity and next-year pricing are subject to RBCC policy in effect at renewal time.
- Renewing partners receive priority over new applicants for the same tier.

*How either party can exit the agreement.*
