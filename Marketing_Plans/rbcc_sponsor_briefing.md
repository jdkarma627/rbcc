## 1. Who We Are

Royal Bengal Cricket Club (RBCC) is a registered Alberta non-profit and one of Edmonton's established Bangladeshi-Canadian cricket organizations. Founded in 2016, we compete in the Edmonton & District Cricket League (EDCL) — a 28+ club league with deep roots in Canadian sporting tradition.

| At a Glance | |
|---|---|

| Founded | 2016 |
| Legal Status | Registered Alberta Non-Profit |
| Active Members | ~38 |
| Teams | 2 (Team A — Premier/1st Division, Team B — 4th/5th Division) |
| League | Edmonton & District Cricket League (EDCL) — 28+ clubs |
| Formats | 40-Over & T20 |
| Community | Bangladeshi-Canadian, with growing diversity |

### Our Mission

"To preserve our cultural heritage and advance cricket in Canada by providing a competitive pathway — specifically promoting the growth of the game within the Bangladeshi community to ensure our traditions and our sport continue for generations."

RBCC is both a competitive cricket organization and a community hub for the Bangladeshi diaspora in Edmonton. This dual role gives us access to partnerships, audiences, and member loyalty that general-purpose sports clubs do not have.
