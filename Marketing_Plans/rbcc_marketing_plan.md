## Competitor Intelligence: What Edmonton Cosmopolitans Do Well

Before diving into the plan, here's what we can learn from the Cosmos and how to adapt it:

| What Cosmos Do | What RBCC Should Take From It |
|---|---|
| **Professional website** with dedicated pages: Who We Are, Events, Gallery, Sponsors, News, Contact | Build an RBCC website with the same page structure — but tighter, more modern |
| **Digital brochure** (hosted on Publuu — a flipbook tool) | Create an RBCC digital brochure — shareable, professional, always up-to-date |
| **Dedicated Sponsors page** that personally names and thanks the Prime Sponsor | We MUST have a sponsors page. Name and celebrate partners publicly — sponsors want recognition |
| **Branded annual event** (Cosmos Mela — 400-500 attendees, multi-day) | RBCC can brand its own flagship event (Gala Night, or a new "Bengal Cup" / "RBCC Mela") |
| **Multi-sport programs** (cricket + kids soccer + softball) | Highlight our members' involvement in other sports (table tennis, tape tennis, etc.) |
| **Community service** (blood drives, food bank, YESS, Santa Anonymous) | Start 1-2 community service initiatives to strengthen the "more than cricket" story |
| **Cross-sport partnership** (Riverhawks — "Baseball meets Cricket") | Explore similar partnerships — visibility to non-cricket audiences |
| **4 social platforms** (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok) | Start with 3 (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn), expand to YouTube for match highlights later |
| **Multi-year prime sponsor deal** (named individual, 3-year commitment) | Push for multi-year commitments — offer a discount for 2-3 year lock-ins |
| **Rich event archive** (Past Events, Recent Events, Gallery) | Document EVERYTHING visually. Every match, every event, every celebration |

## 1. Website

### Why
RBCC has no web presence. In 2026, a club without a website is invisible to potential sponsors, recruits, and media. The website becomes the **permanent home** for the club's story, sponsor logos, event calendar, and contact.

### What We Need

| Page | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Hero banner, mission statement, latest news, next event, sponsor logos |
| **About Us** | Club history, mission, cultural mandate, member demographics |
| **Teams** | Team A & B rosters, player highlights, star player feature (Sid — ex-Canada) |
| **Events** | Upcoming + Past events with photo galleries |
| **Sponsors** | Dedicated sponsor page — logos, names, thank-you messages. Title Partner gets a featured spotlight |
| **Gallery** | Match photos, event photos, tournament moments |
| **News / Blog** | Match reports, tournament recaps, community stories |
| **Contact / Join** | Membership inquiry form, social links, email |

### Platform
- **Recommended:** Wix or Squarespace (low-cost, no-code, professional templates)
- **Cost:** ~$15-25/month
- **Domain:** `royalbengalcc.ca` or `rbcc.ca` (check availability)
- **Timeline:** Live by end of April 2026

### Sponsor Integration on Website
- Sponsor logos in the footer of **every page**
- Title Partner logo in the **header/hero section**
- Dedicated Sponsors page with partner bios and thank-you messages
- Digital brochure hosted as a Publuu flipbook (linked from website + shareable)

## 2. Social Media Strategy

### Platforms (Priority Order)

| Platform | Primary Audience | Content Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Facebook** | Bangladeshi community, families, local businesses, EDCL community | Events, match highlights, community news, sponsor tags, live updates |
| **Instagram** | Younger members, recruits, visual branding | Match-day visuals, player spotlights, stories, reels, professional branding |
| **LinkedIn** | Sponsors, professionals, corporate partners | Club achievements, member professional highlights, sponsorship announcements, community impact |

### Content Calendar — Phase 1 (Weekly)

| Day | Content Type | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Week Ahead — upcoming practice, events, news | FB, IG |
| **Wednesday** | Cricket World — share something happening in the cricketing world (ICC, IPL, CPL, Canada national team) | FB, IG, LinkedIn |
| **Friday** | Club Spotlight — player profile, member achievement, behind-the-scenes, throw-back | FB, IG, LinkedIn |
| **Match Day** | Live match-day post (lineup, score updates, result + photos) | FB, IG |
| **Sunday** | Community corner — share/celebrate other Bangladeshi community events, local business shout-outs | FB |

### Content Calendar — Phase 2 (3x/week → Daily)

Once the cadence is established (after 6-8 weeks), expand to:
- **Daily Instagram stories** (even simple — practice clips, member lunch, match prep)
- **2-3 reels/week** (highlights, practice drills, funny moments)
- **LinkedIn articles** (monthly — club impact, sponsorship value, member profiles)

### Content Pillars

Build every post around one of these pillars. Each serves a strategic purpose.

| # | Pillar | Purpose | Example Post |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Match Day** | Real-time engagement, show the club is active | Lineup graphic, live score, result + celebration photo |
| 2 | **Player Spotlights** | Humanize the club, aid recruitment | "Meet the Bengal" — profile card with stats, job, fun fact |
| 3 | **Cricket World** | Position RBCC as part of the global game | IPL update, ICC ruling, Canada cricket news with RBCC take |
| 4 | **Community** | Show we're more than cricket | Share a Bangladeshi community event, congratulate a local business |
| 5 | **Club Life** | Build FOMO, show culture | Practice vibes, BBQ photos, behind-the-scenes, throwbacks |
| 6 | **Sponsor Appreciation** | Retain sponsors, show value publicly | "Thank you [Sponsor]" post with logo, tag, genuine message |
| 7 | **Multi-Sport** | Show breadth, attract non-cricket followers | Members at table tennis, tape tennis, community volleyball |

### Hook Formulas — *The first line determines if anyone reads the rest*

Use these proven hook structures (adapted from the `social-content` skill) for every post:

**Curiosity Hooks:**
- "5 matches. 0 defeats. Here's how RBCC won the Champions Trophy."
- "The real reason our club is different isn't what you think."
- "$10,000 investment → 52 branded touchpoints. Here's the math."

**Story Hooks:**
- "3 years ago, we almost lost this club. Today, we're champions."
- "Last Saturday, something special happened at the crease."
- "He moved to Edmonton knowing nobody. Cricket changed everything."

**Value Hooks:**
- "How to find your cricket community in Edmonton (without knowing anyone):"
- "5 things every new EDCL player needs to know:"
- "Stop warming the bench. Here's how RBCC develops every player:"

**Contrarian Hooks:**
- "Unpopular opinion: community clubs don't have to choose between fun and competition."
- "Everyone says cricket in Canada is dying. We just won a championship."
- "We stopped doing what every other club does. Here's what happened:"

### Batching Strategy — 2-3 Hours Per Week

1. Review content pillar topics for the week
2. Write 3-5 Facebook posts (text + image/graphic)
3. Create 2-3 Instagram posts + 1 Reel idea
4. Write 1 LinkedIn post
5. Schedule everything (use Meta Business Suite — free)
6. Leave room for real-time match-day content

### Searchable vs Shareable Content

*(From `content-strategy` skill — crucial for deciding what goes on the website vs social)*

| Type | Purpose | Where It Lives | RBCC Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Searchable** | Found via Google/SEO, answers questions, drives traffic long-term | Website (blog/news) | "What is the EDCL?", "Cricket clubs in Edmonton", "How to join a cricket team in Alberta" |
| **Shareable** | Engages emotionally, gets forwarded/shared, builds virality | Social media | Match highlights, player spotlights, championship celebration, behind-the-scenes |

**Rule of thumb:** Website content should be *searchable*. Social content should be *shareable*. Don't mix them up.

### Branding
- **Consistent** logo, colors, fonts across all platforms
- **Professional templates** for match-day graphics, player cards, event posters (use Canva Pro — ~$15/month)
- **Hashtags:** `#RBCC` `#RoyalBengalCC` `#EdmontonCricket` `#EDCL` `#BengalPride`

## 3. Royal Canadian Circus — Sponsor Exposure

This is RBCC's single largest revenue event ($15,000). It's also a **massive untapped sponsor visibility opportunity** — hundreds of people pass through. Here's how to maximize it for sponsors:

### Tactics

| Tactic | Details | Est. Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Branded table cover** | Club/sponsor branded tablecloth at the RBCC volunteer station | $50-100 |
| **Pamphlets / flyers** | Sponsor-branded handouts for circus attendees — "Brought to you by [Sponsor]" | $50-100 (print 500) |
| **Business card display** | Sponsor business cards available at the RBCC station | $0 (sponsor provides) |
| **Stickers** | Small branded stickers (RBCC + sponsor logo) handed out | $30-50 (500 stickers) |
| **Banner stand** | Pull-up banner with RBCC + sponsor branding at the volunteer area | $80-150 (one-time) |
| **Volunteer t-shirts** | RBCC volunteers wear matching branded shirts with sponsor logo (for next season when ordering new gear) | Future budget item |
| **Social media tie-in** | Posts during circus shifts tagging sponsors: "Our team is out tonight volunteering at the Royal Canadian Circus! Powered by [Sponsor]" | $0 |

### Key Messaging
> "RBCC volunteers power the Royal Canadian Circus every year. Our partners make it possible."

This gives the sponsor a **tangible story**: their brand is associated with 30+ volunteers serving the Edmonton community at a major event, multiple nights across the season.

### Ad Copy Formulas

*(From `paid-ads` skill — use these structures for every ad)*

**Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS):**
> New to Edmonton and missing cricket? → Watching from the sidelines while the season passes? → Join RBCC — Edmonton's reigning champions. Practice starts next week. [Join Now]

**Before-After-Bridge (BAB):**
> Before: Playing pickup cricket with strangers on weekends. → After: Competing in the EDCL with a championship-winning squad. → Bridge: RBCC is recruiting for 2026. [Learn More]

**Social Proof Lead:**
> "Undefeated across 5 matches. 6 of the top 25 MVPs. RBCC just won the Champions Trophy." → We're looking for players who want to be part of the next chapter. [Join RBCC]

### Campaign Structure

| Campaign | Objective | Ad Formula | Budget | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Membership Drive** | Recruit new players | BAB + Social Proof | $200 | April–May |
| **Event Promotion** | Promote Gala Night, tournaments, Iftaar | PAS | $100/event | Per event |
| **Sponsor Spotlight** | Boost sponsor appreciation posts | Social Proof Lead | $50/post | Monthly |
| **Tournament Promo** | Promote RBCC-hosted tournaments | PAS + BAB | $150 | Pre-tournament |

### Targeting
- **Location:** Edmonton + surrounding area (Sherwood Park, St. Albert, Spruce Grove)
- **Interests:** Cricket, South Asian community, Bangladeshi culture, EDCL, sports
- **Demographics:** 18-45, male (primary), with secondary campaigns for family events
- **Lookalike audience:** Based on existing Facebook page followers

### Budget: $500-800 for 2026 (pull from $1,000 marketing budget)

## 5. Community Engagement & Cross-Promotion

### Support Bangladeshi Community Events
- Share and promote other Bangladeshi community events on RBCC socials
- Attend community events with RBCC presence (banner, business cards)
- Collaborate on joint events (e.g., community Eid celebration, cultural nights)

### Multi-Sport Visibility
- Highlight members participating in other sports (table tennis, tape tennis, soccer, badminton)
- Post about inter-club friendly tournaments
- Frame RBCC as a **multi-sport community hub**, not just a cricket team

### Community Service (New — Learned from Cosmos)
- **Start small:** 1-2 initiatives per year
- Options: Food bank drive, winter coat drive, blood donation event, youth mentorship
- Benefit: Strengthens "community partner" story, opens doors to government grants, differentiates from pure cricket clubs

## 6. Tournament Hosting

### Plan: Host 1-2 Tournaments Per Year

| Tournament | Format | Prize Money | When | Audience |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **RBCC Bengal Cup** (Flagship) | T10 or T15 tape tennis, 8-10 teams | $500-1,000 | Summer (July/Aug) | EDCL clubs, community teams |
| **Winter Champions Cup** | Indoor tape tennis, 6-8 teams | $300-500 | Winter (Jan/Feb) | Community teams, interprovincial |

### Sponsor Value from Tournaments
- **Title sponsor naming rights** ("The [Sponsor] Bengal Cup")
- Banners and signage at the tournament venue
- Logo on all promotional materials (social media, posters, flyers)
- Mentioned in all tournament-related content
- Photo opportunities with teams and trophies
- Direct engagement with 100-200+ attendees

### Revenue Model
- Team entry fees: $100-200/team × 8-10 teams = $800-2,000
- Tournament sponsorship: $500-2,000
- Food/concession revenue: $200-500
- **Break-even to surplus** with 8+ teams and a title sponsor
